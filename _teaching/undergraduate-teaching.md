English And Communication (Autumn Semester - 2023, 2024, 2025)
======

[Official Course Link](https://ense3.grenoble-inp.fr/fr/formation/smart-systems-5eu9ssy1)

The English and Communication course is designed to help students to do the following:

- Develop their **oral** and **written comprehension** and production skills in English
- Develop their **confidence** in using and communicating in English
- **Professionalise** their English usage and communication
- Develop **soft skills**
- Develop their **cultural** and **intercultural awareness**
- Work towards certification of B2 level according to the CECRL.

At the end of the semester, students are able to:

- Analyse individual learning preferences, and develop strategies to promote **autonomous language learning**
- **Deliver** a prepared text in public with **appropriate techniques** (rhythm, intonation, body language)
- **Communicate** appropriately in a **professional context**, both face-to-face and using telecommunications (telephoning, emailing, video conferencing)
